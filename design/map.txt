The map in "elf" is distributed map.
Everyone can add/delete/modify it.

Distribution:
  All data Should/Must distribute to all countries.

Objects: Point, Line, Area, River, Lake, Way, Building
Applications:
  Download map by BoundingBox or city.
  Get a text description(Country/Province/City/Street..) by a coordinate.
  Find nearby objects.
  Generate game map from the real map.
